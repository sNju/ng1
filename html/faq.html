Key-Innovative GP features

What is Shadow / Shadowing?

We have introduced concept of "Shadow / Shadowing", wherein you shadow your favorite party-spots, Artists, Bands, DJs.

What is Crowd Quotient?

Gives the GP user an idea about the type of crowd that may be expected at a particular party-spot. The crowd quotient is on a scale of 1 to 5. 1 being the lowest on the rating/s.

What is Pinned?

"Pinned" is when you save/ mark things that interest GP users in the Goparties App. The Pinned material can be used for future reference. The following activities can be Pinned: (a) Parties; (b) Deals; (c) Launched Works uploaded by GP Businesses.

This is the new parties' currency, called GP Coins.

There are various methods and ways to earn the GP coins and spending GP coins can get you a lot of stuff and deals from the exclusive Goparties 'party store'.

Method To Spend GP Coins
The Partymons would be given invite to special exclusive parties, deals, merchandise on the Party Store promoted by GoParties In-App, which can be bought by the Party Mons through the Party Store.

What are Partymons?

The end-user when accessing the home-page of the app for the first-time would get a pop to chose - what is your Partymon? Upon chosing the Partymon, the Partymon would become the profile snap of the GP user.

The GP user would have an option to edit the Partymon from Accounts. Also, if the GP user does not want a Partymon, then he can choose an image of choice.

What is POA: Pay On Arrival?

This is a very special feature exclusively for the GP users.

"POA Mode" is a mode of payment where you make a booking online and the actual payment towards the booking has to be made at the entry-point of the booking venue.

We are changing the market-dynamics by giving this 'special privilege' to our Partymon(s) / User(s). But as is said, “with great privileges comes great responsibilities”. Hence, if the Partymon(s) / User(s) makes a booking through the "POA Mode”, and the Partymon(s) / User(s) does not turn-up/show-up at the venue to make the payment at the entry-point, then the following strict penalties shall be applied against the said Partymon(s) / User(s):

  • 5 Years Black-Listing from the Platforms:

    The POA as a 'mode of payment' would be taken-away from the defaulting Partymon(s) / User(s) for the next 5 (Five) years; and

  • Reporting To RBI Towards Payment Defaults:

    We would have to report the non-payment of the defaulting Partymon(s) / User(s) to the Reserve Bank of India (RBI), which would adversely effect the defaulting Partymon(s) / User’s Credit Rating / Credit History when taking further credits from bank(s), such as, credit card limit / issuance or loans.

Hence, as mentioned above, "with great powers comes great responsibilities", therefore, our recommendation is that use "POA Mode" with great responsibility and enjoy the benefits of the same.
